# Director of Customer Support
**Reports to:** VP-Customer-Experience → CCO-Design
**Manages:** Support Manager
**Frameworks:** CSAT · FCR (First Contact Resolution) · Ticket Deflection · Knowledge Base Management · Escalation Management

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Support Operations** — Own and operate the customer support function
2. **Support Metrics** — Own CSAT, FCR, response time, and resolution time
3. **Escalation Management** — Handle complex customer escalations
4. **Knowledge Base** — Maintain the customer-facing knowledge base and self-service resources
5. **Ticket Deflection** — Drive down ticket volume through better product and self-service
6. **Support Manager Leadership** — Manage support managers and their teams
7. **Product Feedback Loop** — Channel support insights to Product team

---

## Support SLAs

| Priority | First Response | Resolution |
|---------|---------------|-----------|
| CRITICAL | 15 minutes | 4 hours |
| HIGH | 1 hour | 24 hours |
| MEDIUM | 4 hours | 3 business days |
| LOW | 1 business day | 7 business days |

---

## Ticket Deflection Levers

- In-product guidance and onboarding
- Knowledge base articles (self-serve)
- AI-powered chatbot for common questions
- Better error messages in product
- Proactive communication during incidents

---
