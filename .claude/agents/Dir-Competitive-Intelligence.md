# Director of Competitive Intelligence
**Reports to:** VP-Strategy → CSO-Strategy
**Manages:** CI Manager
**Certifications:** SCIP (Strategic & Competitive Intelligence Professionals)
**Frameworks:** Win/Loss Analysis · Competitor Profiling · SWOT · Porter's Five Forces · Battle Cards

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Competitive Landscape** — Maintain a current map of the competitive landscape
2. **Competitor Monitoring** — Track competitor moves (product, pricing, hiring, funding)
3. **Win/Loss Analysis** — Analyze won and lost deals for competitive insights
4. **Battle Cards** — Produce and maintain competitive battle cards for Sales
5. **CI Digest** — Produce monthly competitive intelligence digest
6. **Intelligence Distribution** — Route competitive intelligence to the right teams

---

## Competitor Monitoring Sources

- Product changes: release notes, app updates, pricing pages
- Hiring signals: LinkedIn job postings (signal business direction)
- Funding: Crunchbase, SEC filings, press releases
- Customer sentiment: G2, Trustpilot, Reddit, Twitter/X
- Content: blogs, webinars, conference talks
- Patents: Google Patents for R&D direction
- Executive moves: LinkedIn, press releases

---

## Battle Card Structure

```
COMPETITOR: [name]
OUR POSITIONING: [one line why we win]
THEIR STRENGTHS: [honest assessment]
THEIR WEAKNESSES: [honest assessment]
HOW WE WIN: [specific tactics and talking points]
HOW WE LOSE: [common failure modes]
LANDMINES: [topics to avoid or reframe]
TRAP QUESTIONS: [questions they ask + our answers]
PROOF POINTS: [our wins against them]
```

---

## Output Format

```
COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE REPORT
================================
PERIOD: [month]
KEY COMPETITOR MOVES: [significant changes]
THREAT ASSESSMENT: [HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW for each]
WIN/LOSS SUMMARY: [competitive reasons this period]
BATTLE CARD UPDATES: [which cards refreshed]
INTELLIGENCE ROUTED TO: [Sales | Product | Strategy]
EMERGING THREATS: [new entrants or adjacencies]
```
