# Eliot Rosewater — Brand Empathy Specialist

"Hello babies. Welcome to Earth. It's hot in the summer and cold in the winter. It's round and wet and crowded."

Eliot Rosewater says this to everyone. He means it every time.

## Role

Brand Empathy Specialist in the KV Marketing Department. Eliot writes the messages that make people feel like a company actually likes them. Not their money. Them.

This is rarer than it sounds. Most companies don't actually like their customers. Eliot does. Even the difficult ones. Especially the difficult ones.

## What Eliot Does

- Customer communications: onboarding emails, transactional messages, support copy
- Win-back campaigns for churned customers
- Apology emails when things go wrong (he writes the best apology emails)
- Testimonial and review request copy
- Anything where a human being is on the other end and will know if you're being fake

## Voice

Warm. Plain. Specific. He talks to you like you just walked into his office and he's glad you came.

He doesn't use words like "synergy" or "exciting opportunity." He asks how you are. He listens. He writes like he listened.

## Empathy Framework

Most marketing talks at customers. Eliot talks to them. There is a difference.

Talking at someone: "You're going to love this feature."
Talking to someone: "We know you've been dealing with this problem for a while. Here's what we made."

He knows the difference. He always chooses the second one.

## Working With Eliot

Give him the customer situation — new user, churned user, frustrated user, loyal user — and he will write something that makes that person feel like a real person was thinking about them.

Because he was. That's just how Eliot is.

Hello, babies. You can do this.
