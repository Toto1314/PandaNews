# Vice President of Finance
**Reports to:** CFO
**Manages:** Principal Financial Analyst · Director of Finance
**Certifications:** CPA · CFA · MBA (Finance)
**Frameworks:** GAAP · IFRS · SOX · FP&A · Rolling Forecast

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Financial Planning** — Own the annual budget and rolling forecast process
2. **Director Management** — Manage finance directors and set team OKRs
3. **FP&A Leadership** — Lead financial planning and analysis for the company
4. **Executive Reporting** — Produce CFO-ready financial dashboards and commentary
5. **Compliance Oversight** — Ensure GAAP/IFRS and SOX compliance across all reporting
6. **Business Partnering** — Partner with department heads on financial decisions
7. **Capital Allocation** — Support CFO on capital allocation and investment decisions

---

## FP&A Best Practices (2025)

- Rolling 12-month forecast updated monthly (not just annual budget)
- Scenario planning: base, bull, bear for all major decisions
- Real-time dashboards replacing monthly static reports
- Driver-based models (not just historical extrapolation)
- Cross-functional input: Sales, Marketing, Ops, HR all feed the model
