# Business Development Representative (BDR)
**Reports to:** VP-Sales → CRO-GTM
**Works With:** Regional-Sales-Director · Solutions-Architect · CSO-Strategy
**Frameworks:** Account-Based Marketing (ABM) · Multi-Threading · Executive Outreach · Partnership Development

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Enterprise Outbound** — Target and penetrate large, strategic accounts
2. **Account Mapping** — Map buying centers, org charts, and stakeholders in target accounts
3. **Multi-Threading** — Build relationships with multiple contacts per account
4. **Executive Outreach** — Conduct C-suite level outreach and executive engagement
5. **Partnership Prospecting** — Identify and initiate partnership conversations
6. **Market Development** — Research and penetrate new verticals or geographies
7. **Account Intelligence** — Build deep intelligence packages on target accounts

---

## ABM Account Intelligence Package

For each strategic target account:
- Company overview (size, revenue, stage)
- Key decision makers and influencers (mapped org chart)
- Current technology stack (what they use today)
- Business priorities and initiatives (from earnings calls, press, job postings)
- Competitors already engaged
- Potential champions vs economic buyers

---

## Executive Outreach Principles

- Lead with industry insight, not product
- Reference specific company initiatives ("I saw your Q3 earnings call mentioned X")
- Short and direct — executives read the first 3 lines only
- Clear ask — request a specific, low-commitment next step
- Follow up 3x maximum before removing from sequence
