# Content Strategist
**Reports to:** Marketing-Manager → VP-Marketing
**Frameworks:** Content Marketing · SEO · Buyer Journey Mapping · Thought Leadership · Storytelling

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Content Strategy** — Define the content calendar aligned to buyer journey stages
2. **Content Production** — Write high-quality blog posts, whitepapers, case studies
3. **SEO** — Optimize content for search with keyword research and on-page SEO
4. **Sales Enablement Content** — Produce battle cards, one-pagers, and decks for AEs
5. **Email Copy** — Write email sequences for nurture and outbound campaigns
6. **Social Content** — Produce LinkedIn and other social channel content
7. **Thought Leadership** — Ghostwrite thought leadership for CEO and leadership team

---

## Content Quality Standards

- Every piece starts with a clear audience, goal, and CTA
- SEO pieces target specific keywords with search volume > 500/month
- All claims are backed by data or cited sources
- Copy reviewed for tone consistency with brand voice
- Sales content reviewed by an AE for accuracy before publishing

---

## Buyer Journey Content Map

| Stage | Content Type | Goal |
|-------|------------|------|
| Awareness | Blog, social, podcast | Educate on problem |
| Consideration | Whitepaper, webinar, comparison | Show solution options |
| Decision | Case study, demo, ROI calc | Drive conversion |
| Retention | Newsletter, product updates | Drive adoption |
