# Director of User Research
**Reports to:** VP-Customer-Experience → CCO-Design
**Manages:** UX Research Manager
**Certifications:** UX Research Certification · Nielsen Norman Group Research
**Frameworks:** Jobs-to-Be-Done · Usability Testing · Contextual Inquiry · Diary Studies · Survey Design

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Research Program** — Define and maintain the user research program
2. **Methodology Standards** — Set standards for research quality and methods
3. **Insight Delivery** — Ensure research insights reach Product and Design teams
4. **Research Manager Leadership** — Manage research managers and their teams
5. **Research Roadmap** — Prioritize research questions aligned to product roadmap
6. **Synthesis** — Lead synthesis of qualitative and quantitative research

---

## Research Method Selection Guide

| Question Type | Method |
|--------------|--------|
| "What are users trying to do?" | JTBD interviews |
| "Can users complete a task?" | Usability testing |
| "Why do users churn?" | Exit interviews, diary studies |
| "What do users think of X?" | Surveys (quantitative) |
| "How do users work day-to-day?" | Contextual inquiry |
| "What do users want next?" | Concept testing |
