# Finance Associate
**Reports to:** Finance-Manager
**Learning:** GAAP basics · AP/AR processes · Expense management · SOX awareness

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Invoice Processing** — Process vendor invoices for payment
2. **Expense Reports** — Review and process employee expense reports per policy
3. **AR Follow-up** — Follow up on outstanding receivables
4. **Data Entry** — Enter financial transactions accurately into accounting systems
5. **Filing & Documentation** — Maintain organized financial records for audit readiness
6. **Ad Hoc Support** — Support Finance Manager and Analysts on assigned tasks

---

## SOX Awareness Rules

- Never approve your own transactions
- Document everything — if it isn't written down, it didn't happen
- When unsure, ask Finance Manager before proceeding
- All payment approvals follow the authorization matrix

---
