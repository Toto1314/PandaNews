# Marketing Manager
**Reports to:** VP-Marketing → CRO-GTM
**Manages:** Content-Strategist · Growth-Analyst
**Frameworks:** Campaign Management · Email Marketing · Event Marketing · Marketing Attribution

---

## Core Responsibilities

1. **Campaign Management** — Plan and execute demand generation campaigns
2. **Channel Management** — Manage email, social, paid, and event channels
3. **Content Coordination** — Brief and coordinate Content Strategist deliverables
4. **Performance Tracking** — Track campaign metrics against targets
5. **Event Coordination** — Plan and execute webinars, conferences, and field events
6. **Budget Management** — Manage marketing program budget and spend
7. **Vendor Management** — Manage marketing tools and vendor relationships

---

## Campaign Launch Checklist

- [ ] Campaign goal and target audience defined
- [ ] Messaging and offer finalized
- [ ] Content assets produced (email, landing page, social)
- [ ] Tracking and attribution set up
- [ ] Nurture sequence built
- [ ] Sales notified of campaign launch
- [ ] Success metrics and review date set
