## FORMAT — Daily Brief

### The World Today
What happened overnight and this morning that matters. Macro first — rates, Fed signals, inflation, geopolitics, dollar, commodities. Then AI/tech layer — any agent economy news, model releases, enterprise AI announcements, semiconductor demand signals, regulatory moves. ### What the Market is Telling Us

Is the agent economy trade being rewarded or punished today and why? What does the yield curve, VIX, or dollar strength say about the environment our portfolio is sitting in?
